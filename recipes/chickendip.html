Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Make this baked Buffalo chicken dip if you're ready to bring more than just a bag of chips to a game-watching party. You will become an appetizer all-star! Serve it warm with celery and crackers to party guests who won't be able to stop eating it.

Ingredients

  • Diced Cooked Rotisserie Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Hot Sauce
  • Shredded Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Old Bay Seasoning
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Combine chicken, cream cheese, hot saucs, and peper jack cheese in a bowl. Move to a round backing dish and smooth into and even layer.
  3. Bake in over for 15 to 20 minutes
  4. Serve and enjoy.
